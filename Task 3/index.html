Preparing pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Self Raising Flour
  • 2 Cups of Water
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50ml milk (Optional)
  • 30 Grams of Sugar
  • 15 Grams of Salt
  • Cooking Oil

Preparation

  1. Mix the ingredients until it is even
  2. Warm the pan and add some cooking oil
  3. Pour in some mixture and spread evenly
  4. When golden brown, flip the pancake
  5. Add some cooking oil and let it cook till both sides are evenly brown
  6. Repeat this untill all the mixture is used
  7. Serve when warm, with white tea or coffee
~Enjoy ;)
